Development roles:

I (Jeremy Parke) will be working alone and filling all development roles

Need/Rationale/Focus:

The purpose of this site is to promote and explain elements of Celestial Remnants, a Minecraft modpack I spent most my free time developing during my senior year of high school. Information will be provided about various parts of the modpack to try and encourage people to play it.

Elements/Content:

Home
Shows a few images to tease features of the modpack, includes logos, download/curseforge link, etc.
Quests
Explanation of the quest trees and style of questing in the modpack
Items
Explanation of the custom items that have been added and what some of them do
Tinkers'
Explanation of custom materials and how each of them integrates into tinker's construct
Maps
Showcases each of the maps created for the modpack, with a high quality render of each one
Loot bags
Explanation of the loot bag system and showcase of each of the loot bag types
Target Audience:

The target audience is people familiar with the Minecraft modding environment, especially 1.12.2 modding. Ideally people interested in expert-lite quest-based skyblock modpacks.

Design Goals:
I would like the design of the site to be professional-looking but simple, with the goal of the site just being to provide information.

Limiting Factors:
I will most likely be limited by what is taught in this class and what I have time to do. I want to include a lot of written content for the site, which isn't exactly needed to fulfill requirements in this class, so it is a bit of a time sink for little reason.
